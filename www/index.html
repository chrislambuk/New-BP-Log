How To Use.

Enter up to 14 SYS and DIA blood pressure readings from your home monitor. Each reading can be deleted or the app can be reset. The colour codes are:
  • Green = Normal
  • Orange = Mild
  • Red = Severe
Information based on WHO/ISH cardiovascular risk prediction charts
Consult your doctor if you have any concerns about your blood pressure readings.
